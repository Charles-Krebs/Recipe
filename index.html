Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pizza topped with cheese and pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to around 450Â°F.
  2. Spread the dough to make a circle.
  3. Add marinara sauce untill the dough is covered in sauce.
  4. Sprinkle cheese onto the sauce.
  5. Add pepperoni on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza for around 10-15 minutes.
